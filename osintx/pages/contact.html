Frequently Asked Questions

What is your average response time?
We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, our Discord community often provides faster responses from both our team and community members.
Do you offer phone support?
Currently, we provide support through email and Discord. This allows us to better document technical issues and provide detailed assistance. Enterprise customers may have access to priority support channels.
How do I report a bug or technical issue?
Use the contact form above and select "Bug Report". Include details about what you were trying to do, what happened, and any error messages. Screenshots are helpful!
Can I request a feature?
Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Submit your feature request through the contact form or discuss it with the community on Discord.
How can I become a partner?
We welcome partnership inquiries! Use the contact form and select "Partnership Inquiry". Include information about your organization and how you envision working together.
